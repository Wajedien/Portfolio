03 : About me

Who am I

I am a Software Developer in the age of technology. Meaning I have a hunger for knowledge as the world of technology is evergrowing. Growing up surrounded by and fixing hardware while playing around with various software applications has granted me a passion in the field that fuels my interest and keeps me motivated to find the best solutions to any problem presented to me. Developing work I can be proud of while simultaneously meeting business goals and client needs is something I strive to do always.

Software Development Journey So Far

After graduating from Cape Peninsula University of Technology, I shortly started my introduction to the Proffesional world of Software Development as an intern, acting as a sponge, taking in every bit of knowledge I could salvage, I quickly made a name for myself and got offered a permanent position after only 3 months into my internship. Grabbing this oppertunity with both hands I started working directly with clients alongside my seniors and after hitting the 6 month mark I got offered a team lead position and am now leading a team on projects assigned to us.
